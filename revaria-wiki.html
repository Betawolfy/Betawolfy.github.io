Actions in Rêvaria

Last edited:

On Rêvaria, you play as a rêvarian. As a rêvarian, you can perform various actions to gather resources and interact with the world around you.

  • Mining - Extract valuable minerals and ores
  • Fishing - Catch various Fish
  • Harvesting - Not implemented yet.

We'll also let you know probabilities of finding certain resources, and what tools you'll need to perform each action.

Note: Each action requires specific tools and skills. Make sure you have the necessary equipment before attempting any activity.

Mining

Last edited:

Pickaxes and Bonuses

Pickaxe Bonus
Diamond Pickaxe +1.0 (100%)
Gold Pickaxe +0.2 (20%)
Stone Pickaxe +0.1 (10%)

Pity System

  • Counter increases with each common stone obtained
  • Guarantees a rare or legendary reward after 15 stones
  • Counter resets after obtaining a rare or legendary item
Note: The pity system is implemented in the mining system to ensure players get a rare or legendary item after a certain number of common stones. The pity counter resets after obtaining a rare or legendary item.

Base Probabilities (without bonus)

Rarity Chance
Legendary 1%
Rare 5%
Common 94%
Stone or Dog Stone 64%
Note: Stone and common are the same as for now. This might be changed in the future for a more diverse mining experience.

Bonus Probabilities

Pickaxe bonus is applied as a modifier (bonusModifier = pickaxeBonus * 0.1)

Diamond Pickaxe
  • Legendary: 2%
  • Rare: 6%
  • Common: 30%
  • Stone/Dog Stone: 62%
Gold Pickaxe
  • Legendary: 1.2%
  • Rare: 5.2%
  • Common: 30%
  • Stone/Dog Stone: 63.6%
Stone Pickaxe
  • Legendary: 1.1%
  • Rare: 5.1%
  • Common: 30%
  • Stone/Dog Stone: 63.8%
Warning: Please not that these data are subject to change and are only for reference purposes.

Special Case: Dog Stone

  • 10% chance to get a Dog Stone instead of a normal stone
  • Does not increment the pity counter

Durability

  • Each pickaxe has 10 uses
  • Breaks after 10th use
  • Must be replaced after breaking

Fishing

Last edited:

Fishing is an activity where players can catch various aquatic creatures from different water bodies in Rêvaria.

Fishing Probability Analysis

Fishing Rods and Bonuses

Rod Bonus
Enchanted Fishing Rod +0.5 (50%)
Reinforced Fishing Rod +0.15 (15%)
Basic Fishing Rod +0.0 (0%)

Base Probabilities (without bonus)

Rarity Chance
Super Rare Fish 1%
Rare Fish 5%
Common Fish 30%
Nothing 64%

Bonus Probabilities

Fishing rod bonus is applied directly to the chance roll

Enchanted Rod (+50%)
  • Super Rare Fish: 1.5%
  • Rare Fish: 5.5%
  • Common Fish: 30%
  • Nothing: 63%
Reinforced Rod (+15%)
  • Super Rare Fish: 1.15%
  • Rare Fish: 5.15%
  • Common Fish: 30%
  • Nothing: 63.7%
Basic Rod (+0%)
  • Super Rare Fish: 1%
  • Rare Fish: 5%
  • Common Fish: 30%
  • Nothing: 64%

Selling Prices

Fish Price
Super Rare Fish 200
Rare Fish 50
Common Fish 2

Durability

  • Each fishing rod has 10 uses
  • Breaks after 10th use
  • Must be replaced after breaking

Cooldown

  • 1 minute between each fishing attempt
